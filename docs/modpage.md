# Spaturno and Trini Secret Shelter

*A personal pocket-dimension shelter, reached through a painting — a safe, static refuge that tends itself.*

---

## What it is

Hang the **Storditi's hut** painting, right-click it, and step into your own private pocket dimension: a sealed, hand-built shelter that exists outside the world. Inside is a quiet, self-maintaining home — a forest, a hearth, food and drink that never run out — with no monsters, no weather, and nothing that can be broken or lost. When you're done, walk back through the stone arch and you're returned exactly where you left.

It's built on the **Manifold** dimension library and is meant as a cozy, permanent retreat: somewhere to rest, eat, and regroup that always looks exactly the way it was designed.

## How it works

- **Enter:** right-click the *Storditi's hut* painting (an entry portal you place in the world).
- **Return:** walk through the **stone arch** inside the shelter — you reappear at the spot you came from. (`/back` is a fallback.)
- Your **overworld inventory is set aside** on entry and restored on exit — inside the shelter you carry only your worn armour and clothing, so the retreat stays separate from your adventuring kit.

## Features

- 🛡️ **Safe haven** — no damage of any kind inside: mobs, falls, hunger, drowning — all harmless. **Animals can't be hurt either.**
- 🚫 **No spawns** — nothing hostile (or otherwise) ever spawns inside.
- 🌧️ **No weather** — no rain, no phantom storms; torches and fires stay lit. The overworld's weather is untouched.
- 🧱 **Static & tamper-proof** — players can't break, place, drop or steal. The shelter always stays exactly as built.
- 🚪 **Doors work freely**, so you can still move around your home.
- 🍲 **Self-replenishing stores** — take a serving and it quietly comes back ~20 seconds later:
  - barrels, jugs and jars (drinks),
  - storage vessels and crocks (food),
  - a shelf of bowls, fruit set out on tables,
  - **cuttable cheese & pie** that regrow the slice you cut.
- 🔥 **An ever-lit firepit** — the firewood never runs out.
- 🎵 **A usable resonator** — play music with tuning cylinders.
- 🍽️ **Set out & pick up bowls** on tables as you like.
- 💤 **Sleep safely** — nap in a bed and wake up right where you slept, in an intact shelter.
- 🛏️ **Survives relogs** — leave and rejoin and the shelter is restored around you.

## Requirements

- Vintage Story **1.22+**
- **[Manifold](https://mods.vintagestory.at/manifold)** (hard dependency)

## Notes

- The shelter's interior is a schematic, so its layout is fixed and curated.
- Everything inside is read-only by design — it's a refuge, not a build plot.
